Our Mission

Called to Serve,
Ordained to Lead

Melchizedek Order Ministries International is a global community of believers united in the ancient and eternal priesthood—an order not of lineage, but of divine appointment. We exist to equip the saints, to proclaim the Kingdom, and to walk in the fullness of our high-priestly calling through Christ Jesus.

"For this Melchizedek, king of Salem, priest of the Most High God, met Abraham returning from the slaughter of the kings and blessed him."

Hebrews 7:1
  • I
    Intercession Standing in the gap through prayer, lifting every nation and people before the throne of grace.
  • II
    Righteousness & Peace Embodying the twin pillars of Salem—righteousness in conduct, peace in proclamation.
  • III
    Discipleship Raising up whole-life disciples who walk in authority, humility, and supernatural power.
  • IV
    Global Outreach Carrying the Gospel without borders, with compassion for the poor, the lost, and the overlooked.

The Order of Melchizedek

A Priesthood Without End

"King of Righteousness. King of Peace. Priest of the Most High God."

Melchizedek stands as one of Scripture's most profound mysteries—a priest-king who blessed Abraham, received his tithe, and foreshadowed the eternal priesthood of Jesus Christ. The Letter to the Hebrews reveals that Christ holds His office permanently, by the power of an indestructible life, in this same order.

Melchizedek Order Ministries International was born from the conviction that the Church is called to walk in that same priestly anointing—to offer spiritual sacrifices, to bless and not curse, to stand as mediators between heaven and earth. Our name is our identity: royal, holy, set apart, yet fully engaged with the world God so loves.
